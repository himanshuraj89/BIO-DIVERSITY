About us

Welcome to SHIV FOUNDATION

At SHIV FOUNDATION , we are passionate about conserving biodiversity and preserving the rich tapestry of life on our planet. We are promoting awareness, conducting research, and implementing conservation strategies. Our mission is to safeguard ecosystems, protect endangered species, and promote a harmonious coexistence between humans and the natural world.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every species, from the smallest pollinator to the mightiest predator, thrives in its natural habitat. A world where the delicate balance of life is respected and upheld for the well-being of future generations.

Our mission

Our primary goal is to protect and restore biodiversity through a combination of research, education, and on-ground conservation efforts.
